# DMCA Policy

We comply with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (17 U.S.C. §512). To report alleged infringement, send a notice that satisfies §512(c)(3) to our designated agent listed in our [Terms](/terms#dmca). We also maintain and reasonably implement a policy for termination of repeat infringers per §512(i). You can verify designated agents at the U.S. Copyright Office’s directory.

> Resources: U.S. Copyright Office DMCA Designated Agent Directory; 17 U.S.C. §512.
